Hyderabad Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo chicken pieces
  • 4 medium onions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons garlic ginger paste
  • 4 tablespoons biryani mix
  • 4 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • A bunch of coriander
  • 5 green chillies
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 3 pieces cloves
  • 3 cups basmati rice
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Sliced lemons for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a pan at medium heat, add some oil and cook the onions for 10 minutes. Add garlic ginger paste and fry for 2 minutes before adding the chicken pieces. Cook for 2 -3 minutes at high heat.
  2. Once the chicken changes in color, add in the biryani mix, give it a good stir. Splash in some water, place in the tomatoes and mix.
  3. Pour in the yogurt and give it a mix. Sprinkle in coriander and green chillies. Cover and cook for 20 minutes. Let it cook till the sauce has almost dried up. Turn off the heat and set aside.
  4. For the rice; In a pot of boiling water add some salt, cinnamon sticks, and cloves. Mix till salt dissolves. Add in the basmati rice and let this cook till it's 90% done.
  5. After its cooked, drain the rice. Place half of these in a pot, layering the cooked chicken on top. Then, cover the chicken with the remaining rice. Top with coriander.
  6. Cover and cook for 7 - 8 minutes. Mix to make sure the rice is coated with the sauce. Transfer to a plate and serve garnished with lemon slices.

Alfredo pasta

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine pasta - 250g
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic (minced) - 2 cloves
  • Heavy cream - 1 cup (240ml)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated) - 1 cup (90g)
  • Salt - ½ tsp (or to taste)
  • Black pepper - ¼ tsp
  • Nutmeg (optional) - a pinch
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish) - 1 tbsp

Procedure:

  1. Cook the Pasta - Boil fettuccine in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Melt Butter - In a pan, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Add Garlic & Cream - Sauté minced garlic briefly, then pour in heavy cream and simmer.
  4. Mix in Cheese - Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  5. Season - Add salt, black pepper, and optional nutmeg for flavor.
  6. Combine & Serve - Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce, garnish with parsley, and serve hot.

Milk Dalia

Ingredients:

  • Dalia (Broken Wheat) - ½ cup
  • Ghee - 1 tsp
  • Water - 1 cup
  • Milk - 1.5 cups
  • Sugar/Jaggery - 2 tbsp (adjust to taste)
  • Cardamom Powder - ¼ tsp (optional)
  • Chopped Nuts (Almonds, Cashews, Pistachios) - 1 tbsp (optional)
  • Raisins - 1 tbsp (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Roast Dalia - Heat 1/2 cup broken wheat (dalia) in 1 tsp ghee until golden brown.
  2. Boil Water - Add 1 cup water and cook on low flame until dalia softens.
  3. Add Milk - Pour in 1.5 cups of milk and mix well.
  4. Sweeten - Add 2 tbsp sugar or jaggery and stir.
  5. Enhance Flavor - Add cardamom powder, chopped nuts, or raisins.
  6. Serve - Simmer for 2-3 minutes, then serve warm.
